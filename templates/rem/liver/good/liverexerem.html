Liver Exercise Recommendations

1. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Stimulates liver function and promotes detoxification.

2. Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)

Improves blood flow to the liver, enhancing its health.

3. Naukasana (Boat Pose)

Strengthens the liver by stimulating abdominal organs.

4. Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Spinal Twist Pose)

Promotes liver detoxification and relieves congestion.

5. Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend)

Stimulates liver function and aids in digestion.

6. Matsyasana (Fish Pose)

Improves liver efficiency by stretching the abdomen.

7. Kapotasana (Pigeon Pose)

Enhances blood circulation to the liver and supports detox.

8. Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing)

Balances oxygen flow, supporting liver health and detoxification.

9. Kapalabhati (Skull Shining Breathing)

Boosts metabolism and helps cleanse the liver of toxins.

10. Brisk or Light Walking

Encourages overall liver function by improving circulation and metabolism.
