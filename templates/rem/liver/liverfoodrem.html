Leafy Greens & Cruciferous Vegetables

  • Rich in antioxidants and phytochemicals that support liver detoxification
  • Examples: spinach, kale, broccoli, Brussels sprouts
  • Help reduce inflammation and support healthy liver function
  • Incorporate daily to support liver health

Whole Grains

  • Provide essential nutrients and fiber, aiding liver health
  • Examples: brown rice, oats, quinoa, whole-grain bread and pasta
  • Support healthy blood sugar levels and detoxification processes
  • Limit refined grains like white rice and white bread

Healthy Fats

  • Omega-3 fatty acids and monounsaturated fats reduce liver fat accumulation
  • Sources: fatty fish (salmon, mackerel), olive oil, avocados, nuts, and seeds
  • Support liver function and reduce inflammation
  • Avoid trans fats and limit saturated fats

Lean Proteins

  • Important for tissue repair and liver regeneration
  • Sources: lean meats, poultry, fish, legumes, tofu, and eggs
  • Help maintain liver health and assist in detoxification
  • Limit red meat and processed meats

Antioxidants

  • Combat oxidative stress and protect liver cells
  • Sources: berries, oranges, nuts, seeds, green tea
  • Support liver function and detoxification pathways
  • Include daily for optimal liver health

Limit Sugary & Processed Foods

  • Excess sugar and processed foods can contribute to fatty liver
  • Avoid sugary drinks, processed snacks, and fast foods
  • Opt for whole, unprocessed foods to support liver health

Hydration

  • Drink plenty of water to aid in detoxification
  • Support liver functions by keeping the body hydrated
  • Limit excessive alcohol consumption

Additional Tips

  • Consult a dietitian for personalized liver health advice
  • Limit alcohol consumption to protect liver function
  • Stay active with regular exercise
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques
