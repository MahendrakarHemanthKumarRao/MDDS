Recommended Exercises

Tadasana (Mountain Pose): Improves posture and promotes blood circulation.

Vrikshasana (Tree Pose): Enhances balance and strengthens the heart.

Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Dog Pose): Boosts circulation and relaxes the heart muscles.

Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Opens the chest, improving lung and heart function.

Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose): Strengthens the heart by increasing blood flow.

Uttanasana (Standing Forward Bend): Relieves stress and improves heart rate.

Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing): Balances blood pressure and calms the mind.

Shavasana (Corpse Pose): Reduces stress and promotes overall heart relaxation.

Walking (Brisk or Casual): Enhances cardiovascular endurance and lowers blood pressure.

Pranayama (Deep Breathing Exercises): Increases oxygen supply and strengthens the respiratory system.
