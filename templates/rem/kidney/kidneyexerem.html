Yoga Poses for Kidney Health

Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose): Stimulates kidney function and improves blood circulation to the abdominal region.
Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Spinal Twist Pose): Promotes detoxification and improves kidney efficiency.
Paschimottanasana (Seated Forward Bend): Enhances kidney function by stimulating the abdominal organs.
Dhanurasana (Bow Pose): Activates the kidneys and helps eliminate toxins.
Malasana (Garland Pose): Improves digestion and stimulates kidney and bladder function.
Ustrasana (Camel Pose): Opens the abdominal area, boosting kidney energy and function.
Shalabhasana (Locust Pose): Strengthens the lower back and stimulates the kidneys.
Kapalabhati (Skull Shining Breathing): Detoxifies the body and promotes better kidney health.
Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing): Balances body fluids and improves overall kidney function.
Walking: Enhances blood flow to the kidneys and supports natural detoxification.
