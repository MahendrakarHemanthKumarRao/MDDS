Fruits and Vegetables

  • Fresh fruits: Apples, berries, melons
  • Diverse vegetables: Carrots, sweet potatoes, bell peppers

Proteins

  • Lean meats, eggs, fish (rich in omega-3 fatty acids)
  • Plant proteins: Chickpeas, quinoa, tofu, or nuts

Grains

  • Whole grains like brown rice, whole wheat, quinoa
  • Healthy cereals with minimal sugar

Healthy Fats

  • Avocados, olive oil, nuts, and seeds

Dairy or Alternatives

  • Milk, yogurt, cheese, or plant-based alternatives like almond milk

Hydration

  • Water, fresh fruit juices, or infused water

Immune Support Foods

  • Garlic, ginger, and honey

Additional Tips

  • Consult a dietitian for personalized nutrition advice.
  • Limit alcohol consumption to protect liver function.
  • Stay active with regular exercise.
  • Manage stress through relaxation techniques.
