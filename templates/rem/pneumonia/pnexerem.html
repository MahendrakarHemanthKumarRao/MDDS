Pneumonia Exercise Recommendations

1. Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Expands the chest, improving lung capacity and oxygen intake.

2. Matsyasana (Fish Pose)

Opens up the respiratory tract and reduces lung congestion.

3. Ardha Matsyendrasana (Half Spinal Twist Pose)

Enhances lung function and supports easier breathing.

4. Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)

Strengthens respiratory muscles and boosts lung efficiency.

5. Tadasana (Mountain Pose)

Improves posture and helps open the airways for better breathing.

6. Ustrasana (Camel Pose)

Expands the chest cavity and increases oxygen flow to the lungs.

7. Pranayama (Deep Breathing Exercises)

Improves oxygen levels and enhances overall lung health.

8. Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing)

Reduces respiratory discomfort and promotes steady breathing.

9. Kapalabhati (Skull Shining Breathing)

Detoxifies the lungs and improves airflow, clearing mucus.

10. Walking (Light to Moderate)

Improves circulation and encourages better lung function during recovery.
