Exercise Recommendations for Breast Cancer

Physical activity plays a crucial role in the recovery and overall well-being of individuals diagnosed with breast cancer. Below are some recommended exercises that can support the healing process:

Sirsasana (Headstand)

Improves blood flow to the brain, enhancing oxygen supply and reducing stress.

Sarvangasana (Shoulder Stand)

Boosts circulation to the head, improving brain function and detoxification.

Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose)

Stimulates the thyroid and improves blood flow to the brain and neck area.

Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Dog Pose)

Increases oxygen flow to the brain, supporting mental clarity and focus.

Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)

Enhances circulation to the brain and promotes overall well-being.

Dhanurasana (Bow Pose)

Opens up the chest and stimulates the brain by improving oxygenation.

Bhramari Pranayama (Bee Breath)

Calms the mind and relieves mental stress, promoting relaxation and brain function.

Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing)

Balances the brain hemispheres, reducing anxiety and improving concentration.

Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)

Relieves tension in the neck and shoulders while improving blood flow to the brain.

Shavasana (Corpse Pose)

Relieves stress, helps clear the mind, and promotes deep relaxation.
